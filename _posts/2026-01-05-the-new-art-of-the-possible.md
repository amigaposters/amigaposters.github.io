Here’s my first poster for 2026 — an homage to the **Commodore Amiga 2000**. It's based on a TV commercial for the classic big box Amiga produced in the 80s.
<br><br>
The A2000 was a computer you built into. Designed for expansion, it offered a platform that invited upgrades, experiments, and ambitious workflows, covering video, music, graphics, animation, and productivity, all on the same desk. This poster is a small tribute to a machine that made “endless possibilities” feel less like a slogan—and more like a design philosophy.<br><br>
